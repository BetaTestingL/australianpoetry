## Resources for Students and Teachers

### How to Read a Poem (Step-by-Step Guide)
1. Read it once without stopping — get the flow.
2. Read it again, slowly — note imagery and tone.
3. Identify the speaker and themes.
4. Annotate key lines and devices.
5. Summarise the poem in 2–3 sentences.

### Classroom Prompts
- Write a poem about a place that shaped you.
- Describe a landscape using only sound-based imagery.
